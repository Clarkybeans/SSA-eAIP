## Phraseology

Due to all SIDs being suspended, all pilots are to get Runway Track with an initial climb of FL90.

!!! info "Delivery Phraseology"
    "(callsign), cleared to (destination), FLxxx on request, after departure Runway XX, non standard, maintain runway track, climb to FL90, once passing 2000ft, contact Approach on 119.7, squawk (squawk)"

If no Approach or Area controller is online, you may omit the auto handover from the clearance or replace it with "Monitor Advisory on 122.8".
